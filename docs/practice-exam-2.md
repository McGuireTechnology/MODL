## Full Practice Test 2

<quiz>
What is the minimum age to obtain a full (unrestricted) Class F driver license in Missouri?
- [ ] 16 years old
- [ ] 17 years old
- [x] 18 years old
- [ ] 21 years old

You must be 18 years old to obtain a full, unrestricted Class F driver license in Missouri.
</quiz>

<quiz>
During the Intermediate License stage, drivers under 18 cannot drive between what hours?
- [ ] 10 PM and 5 AM
- [ ] 11 PM and 6 AM
- [x] 1 AM and 5 AM
- [ ] Midnight and 4 AM

Intermediate license holders cannot drive between 1 AM and 5 AM unless for work, school, or emergencies.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What shape is a yield sign?
- [ ] Circle
- [ ] Octagon
- [x] Triangle pointing down
- [ ] Diamond

A yield sign is a downward-pointing triangle (inverted triangle).
</quiz>

<quiz>
An orange diamond-shaped sign indicates:
- [ ] School zone
- [x] Work zone
- [ ] Railroad crossing
- [ ] No passing zone

Orange signs indicate construction or maintenance work zones.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What does a flashing yellow traffic signal mean?
- [ ] Stop, then proceed when safe
- [x] Slow down and proceed with caution
- [ ] Prepare to stop
- [ ] Yield to cross traffic

Flashing yellow means proceed with caution - slow down and be prepared to stop if necessary.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A broken white line between lanes means:
- [ ] No lane changes allowed
- [x] Lane changes permitted when safe
- [ ] Exit lane only
- [ ] Carpool lane

Broken white lines separate lanes moving in the same direction and indicate lane changes are permitted when safe.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What does a steady yellow traffic light mean?
- [ ] Speed up to get through the intersection
- [ ] Stop immediately
- [x] Prepare to stop; the light is about to turn red
- [ ] Proceed with caution

Yellow means the light is about to turn red - stop if you can do so safely.
</quiz>

<quiz>
At a four-way stop, if four vehicles arrive simultaneously, who goes first?
- [ ] The largest vehicle
- [ ] The vehicle that flashes its lights
- [x] Drivers should use courtesy and eye contact to determine who goes first
- [ ] The vehicle going straight

When all arrive at the same time, drivers should use courtesy, eye contact, and common sense to determine right-of-way.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What is the maximum speed limit on Missouri interstate highways (unless posted otherwise)?
- [ ] 55 mph
- [ ] 65 mph
- [x] 70 mph
- [ ] 75 mph

Missouri's maximum speed limit on rural interstates is 70 mph unless otherwise posted.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When changing lanes, you should:
- [ ] Signal, check mirrors, turn
- [x] Check mirrors, signal, check blind spot, change lanes when safe
- [ ] Signal and change lanes immediately
- [ ] Honk your horn and change lanes

Proper sequence: check mirrors, signal, check blind spot, then change lanes when safe.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You should dim your headlights within ____ feet when following another vehicle:
- [ ] 100 feet
- [ ] 200 feet
- [x] 300 feet
- [ ] 500 feet

Dim your high beams within 300 feet when following another vehicle.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When parallel parking uphill with a curb, turn your wheels:
- [x] Away from the curb
- [ ] Toward the curb
- [ ] Straight
- [ ] Either direction

When parking uphill WITH a curb, turn wheels away from the curb so the car rolls backward into the curb if brakes fail.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You must not park within ____ feet of a crosswalk:
- [ ] 10 feet
- [ ] 15 feet
- [x] 20 feet
- [ ] 25 feet

Do not park within 20 feet of a crosswalk at an intersection.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When exiting a highway, you should:
- [ ] Speed up on the exit ramp
- [ ] Slow down in the right lane before the exit
- [x] Move into the deceleration lane and then slow down
- [ ] Stop before entering the exit ramp

Use the deceleration (exit) lane to slow down - don't slow in the through lane.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What does a white X over a highway lane mean?
- [ ] Lane is ending
- [ ] High occupancy vehicle lane
- [x] Lane is closed - do not drive in this lane
- [ ] Construction zone ahead

A white X means the lane is closed to traffic - you must not drive in that lane.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You must stop for a school bus when:
- [ ] Its yellow lights are flashing
- [x] Its red lights are flashing and stop arm is extended
- [ ] It is stopped at an intersection
- [ ] All of the above

You must stop when the red lights are flashing and the stop arm is extended.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A pedestrian using a white cane or guide dog:
- [ ] Has the right-of-way in all situations
- [ ] Should wait for traffic to clear
- [x] Is blind or visually impaired and must be given right-of-way
- [ ] Is requesting assistance

A white cane or guide dog indicates the person is blind or visually impaired and must always be given right-of-way.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When driving in rain, you should:
- [ ] Increase speed to get through it quickly
- [ ] Use cruise control to maintain steady speed
- [x] Reduce speed and increase following distance
- [ ] Turn on hazard lights

Rain reduces traction and visibility - slow down and increase following distance.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Black ice is most likely to form:
- [ ] In the middle of the day
- [x] On bridges and overpasses
- [ ] In sunny areas
- [ ] On uphill slopes only

Bridges and overpasses freeze first because cold air circulates above and below them.
</quiz>

<quiz>
The legal blood alcohol concentration (BAC) limit for drivers 21 and over is:
- [ ] 0.02%
- [ ] 0.05%
- [x] 0.08%
- [ ] 0.10%

For drivers 21 and older, the legal BAC limit is 0.08%.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Using a cell phone while driving (for any purpose other than emergency):
- [ ] Is legal for experienced drivers
- [ ] Is legal with hands-free device
- [x] Texting is illegal for all drivers
- [ ] Is only illegal for drivers under 21

Texting while driving is illegal for all Missouri drivers. However, talking on a phone (hands-free or handheld) is currently legal for drivers 22 and over.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Accumulating 12 points in 12 months results in:
- [ ] Warning letter
- [x] License suspension
- [ ] Mandatory traffic school
- [ ] Increased insurance rates only

12 points in 12 months (or 18 points in 24 months, or 8 points in 18 months) results in license suspension.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Running a red light results in how many points?
- [x] 2 points
- [ ] 3 points
- [ ] 4 points
- [ ] 6 points

Running a red light or stop sign is 2 points.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Child safety seats are required for children under what age?
- [ ] 4 years old
- [ ] 6 years old
- [x] 8 years old
- [ ] 10 years old

Missouri law requires appropriate child restraints for children under 8 years old (or under 80 pounds).
</quiz>

<quiz>
When should you check your blind spots?
- [ ] Only when changing lanes
- [x] Before changing lanes and when merging
- [ ] Every 5 seconds
- [ ] Only when backing up

Always check blind spots before changing lanes and when merging onto highways.
</quiz>
