## Scenario / Think & Reveal

<details><summary>1. Highway speed; reading a long message.</summary>
Pull over safely if urgent; avoid reading while moving.
</details>

<details><summary>2. Night drive + fatigue + notifications.</summary>
Silence alerts; increase following distance; consider rest break.
</details>

<details><summary>3. Pet loose in vehicle.</summary>
Safely restrain pet before trip to prevent sudden distraction.
</details>

<details><summary>4. Complex audio adjustments needed.</summary>
Set playlist & volume pre-trip; avoid multi-step adjustments mid-drive.
</details>

<details><summary>5. Feeling mentally preoccupied.</summary>
Pause driving; refocus or delay trip if concentration low.
</details>

Reinforce layered mitigation planning.
