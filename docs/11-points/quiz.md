## Scenario / Think & Reveal

<details><summary>1. Record shows rising points from distraction-related violations. Plan?</summary>
Implement phone usage discipline: hands-free only, pre-set navigation, focused scanning routine.
</details>

<details><summary>2. Insurance premium spiked after point accumulation. Response?</summary>
Adopt strict defensive habits, consider approved driver improvement course.
</details>

<details><summary>3. Received notice of potential suspension threshold. Action?</summary>

</details>

<details><summary>4. Frequent late departures causing rushing. Strategy?</summary>
Shift schedule earlier; create pre-drive checklist to reduce last-minute scramble.
</details>

<details><summary>5. Minor equipment violation led to points. Prevention?</summary>
Regular pre-trip inspection for lights, signals, tires.
</details>
