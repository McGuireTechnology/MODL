## Scenario / Think & Reveal

<details><summary>1. Opposite side of divided highway bus with red lights. Stop?</summary>
Likely exempt if physical median present—verify state rule; on undivided road you must stop.
</details>

<details><summary>2. Cyclist signals left, traffic moderate. Your action?</summary>
Allow lane change, slow slightly, avoid overtaking during maneuver.
</details>

<details><summary>3. Motorcycle ahead with constant turn signal. Interpretation?</summary>
Do not assume turn; maintain gap until movement confirms.
</details>

<details><summary>4. Large truck begins wide right turn from left lane. Response?</summary>
Do not squeeze right; yield space until turn completes.
</details>

<details><summary>5. Emergency vehicle in multi-lane congestion behind you. Strategy?</summary>
Coordinate gradual lane shift right with surrounding drivers; avoid abrupt stop mid-lane.
</details>
