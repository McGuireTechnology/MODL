## Scenario / Think & Reveal (Open‑Ended)

Think about each scenario, then reveal a suggested answer.

<details><summary>1. Name mismatch after marriage — What do you do?</summary>
Update your name with the Social Security Administration so documents align, then bring required proofs (identity, SSN, residency) for your application.
</details>

<details><summary>2. You moved within Missouri — What are your obligations?</summary>
Notify the licensing authority of the address change within the mandated timeframe; update insurance and registration records as needed.
</details>

<details><summary>3. Preparing for the knowledge exam — Two topic areas?</summary>
Study rules of the road, traffic signs/ signals / markings, right‑of‑way, speed management, and safe driving practices.
</details>

<details><summary>4. Road test behaviors — Name at least three.</summary>
Vehicle control, observation/scanning, signaling, lane positioning, speed management, right‑of‑way compliance, and legal adherence.
</details>

<details><summary>5. REAL ID vs non‑REAL ID — Key deciding question?</summary>
Will you need the license for domestic air travel or access to certain federal facilities? If yes, choose REAL ID.
</details>
