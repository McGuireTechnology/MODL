## Scenario / Think & Reveal

<details><summary>1. Feeling drowsy 1 hour into drive; next rest area 10 miles. Plan?</summary>
Increase ventilation, brief mild stimulation, reach rest area; if severe, stop safely sooner.
</details>

<details><summary>2. New prescription with drowsiness warning and early appointment. Choice?</summary>
Arrange ride or delay driving until effects assessed.
</details>

<details><summary>3. Emotional upset after argument. Drive now?</summary>
Delay trip; use calming routine until focus restored.
</details>

<details><summary>4. Friend insists they are fine after drinking. Response?</summary>
Offer alternative transport; do not allow impaired driving.
</details>

<details><summary>5. Long overnight drive planned. Preparation?</summary>
Full sleep, plan breaks every ~2 hours, avoid heavy meal before departure.
</details>
