## Full Practice Test 3

<quiz>
How many hours of behind-the-wheel training are required for drivers under 18 to obtain a license?
- [ ] 20 hours
- [ ] 30 hours
- [x] 40 hours
- [ ] 50 hours

Missouri requires 40 hours of behind-the-wheel training (including 10 hours at night) for drivers under 18.
</quiz>

<quiz>
With an Instruction Permit, you may drive when accompanied by:
- [ ] Any licensed driver
- [x] A licensed driver 21 or older in the front seat
- [ ] Your instructor only
- [ ] Anyone with a permit

Instruction Permit holders must have a licensed driver 21 or older seated in the front passenger seat.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A red octagonal sign means:
- [ ] Yield
- [x] Stop
- [ ] Do not enter
- [ ] Wrong way

An octagon (8-sided) red sign is always a STOP sign.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A diamond-shaped yellow sign with a black 'X' and 'RR' indicates:
- [ ] Construction zone
- [x] Railroad crossing ahead
- [ ] Intersection ahead
- [ ] School zone

The yellow diamond with X and RR warns of a railroad crossing ahead.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A green traffic light means:
- [ ] Go without looking
- [ ] Speed up
- [x] Proceed when it is safe to do so
- [ ] You have the absolute right-of-way

Green means go when it is safe - you must still yield to pedestrians and vehicles already in the intersection.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A solid yellow line on your side of the center line means:
- [ ] Passing is permitted
- [x] Passing is not permitted
- [ ] No parking
- [ ] Lane ends ahead

A solid yellow line on your side means you cannot pass. Passing is only allowed when you have a broken line on your side.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What does a red arrow on a traffic signal mean?
- [ ] Yield before turning in the direction of the arrow
- [ ] You may turn after stopping
- [x] You cannot turn in the direction of the arrow
- [ ] Proceed with caution in the direction of the arrow

A red arrow means you must NOT turn in that direction - wait for a green signal.
</quiz>

<quiz>
If two vehicles arrive at an intersection with stop signs at the same time, who has the right-of-way?
- [ ] The larger vehicle
- [ ] The vehicle on the left
- [x] The vehicle on the right
- [ ] The vehicle going straight

When two vehicles arrive simultaneously, the vehicle on the RIGHT has the right-of-way.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What is Missouri's 'Move Over Law'?
- [ ] Change lanes for any slow vehicle
- [x] Move over or slow down for stopped emergency vehicles with flashing lights
- [ ] Pull over for all emergency vehicles
- [ ] Change lanes at intersections

Missouri's Move Over Law requires drivers to move over a lane (or slow down if unable to move over) for stopped emergency vehicles with flashing lights.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You should signal at least ____ feet before turning in urban areas:
- [ ] 50 feet
- [ ] 75 feet
- [x] 100 feet
- [ ] 150 feet

Signal at least 100 feet before turning in urban areas (city/town).
</quiz>

<quiz>
When should you use high beam headlights?
- [ ] Always at night
- [ ] In city traffic
- [x] On open highways when there is no oncoming traffic
- [ ] In fog

Use high beams on open highways when there is no oncoming or nearby traffic. Dim them within 500 feet of oncoming traffic and 300 feet when following.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When parallel parking downhill, you should turn your wheels:
- [ ] Away from the curb
- [x] Toward the curb
- [ ] Straight ahead
- [ ] It doesn't matter

When parking downhill, turn wheels toward the curb so the car will roll into the curb if brakes fail.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You must not park within ____ feet of a fire hydrant:
- [ ] 5 feet
- [x] 10 feet
- [ ] 15 feet
- [ ] 20 feet

Never park within 10 feet of a fire hydrant.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When entering a highway from an entrance ramp, you should:
- [ ] Stop and wait for an opening
- [ ] Force your way into traffic
- [x] Match the speed of highway traffic and merge when safe
- [ ] Enter slowly and speed up after merging

Use the acceleration lane to match highway speed, then merge smoothly when there's a safe gap.
</quiz>

<quiz>
On a multi-lane highway, which lane should slower traffic use?
- [ ] Left lane
- [ ] Middle lane
- [x] Right lane
- [ ] Any lane

Slower traffic should keep right. Use left lanes for passing.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When a school bus is stopped with flashing red lights on a two-lane road, drivers must:
- [ ] Stop only if traveling in the same direction
- [x] Stop in both directions
- [ ] Slow down and proceed with caution
- [ ] Change lanes

On a two-lane road, traffic in BOTH directions must stop for a school bus with flashing red lights.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When sharing the road with motorcycles, you should:
- [ ] Assume they will move out of your way
- [ ] Give them less space since they're smaller
- [x] Give them a full lane width
- [ ] Drive close to encourage them to speed up

Motorcycles are entitled to a full lane width - don't share the lane with them.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Hydroplaning occurs when:
- [ ] Your tires are new
- [ ] You drive too slow in rain
- [x] Water builds up between your tires and the road surface
- [ ] Your windshield wipers fail

Hydroplaning happens when water builds up faster than your tires can push it away, causing you to lose traction.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When driving in fog, you should:
- [ ] Use high beam headlights
- [x] Use low beam headlights and fog lights if equipped
- [ ] Turn off all lights
- [ ] Use hazard lights

Use low beams in fog - high beams reflect back and reduce visibility. Use fog lights if your vehicle has them.
</quiz>

<quiz>
For drivers under 21, Missouri has a Zero Tolerance law with a BAC limit of:
- [ ] 0.00%
- [x] 0.02%
- [ ] 0.05%
- [ ] 0.08%

Missouri has Zero Tolerance for drivers under 21 - any measurable BAC (0.02% or higher) results in penalties.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Implied consent means:
- [ ] You consent to obey traffic laws
- [x] By operating a vehicle, you consent to chemical testing if arrested for DWI
- [ ] You consent to allow police to search your vehicle
- [ ] You consent to vehicle insurance requirements

Missouri's Implied Consent law means by driving, you automatically consent to chemical testing (breath, blood, urine) if arrested for DWI.
</quiz>

<quiz>
How many points does speeding result in?
- [ ] 2 points
- [x] 3 points
- [ ] 4 points
- [ ] It depends on how fast over the limit

Speeding violations are 3 points in Missouri.
</quiz>

<quiz>
If you accumulate 8 points in 18 months, what happens?
- [ ] Nothing
- [ ] Warning letter
- [x] 30-day license suspension
- [ ] 1-year license revocation

8 points in 18 months results in a 30-day license suspension.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Your vehicle's horn should be used:
- [x] To alert other drivers of danger
- [ ] To express anger
- [ ] To greet friends
- [ ] Whenever you want

Use your horn only to alert others of danger or to avoid a collision.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Tire tread depth should be at least:
- [ ] 1/16 inch
- [ ] 1/8 inch
- [x] 2/32 inch
- [ ] 1/4 inch

Missouri law requires at least 2/32 inch of tire tread depth (use the penny test - Lincoln's head should be partially covered).
</quiz>
