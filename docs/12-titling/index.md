# Titling & Registration (Chapter 12)

!!! abstract "TL;DR"
    Title proves ownership; registration authorizes road use—keep documents current and renew on time to avoid penalties.

## Scope

Core concepts of vehicle titling and registration: purpose, required documentation, renewal considerations. Verify Missouri-specific forms and intervals.

## Learning Objectives

- Distinguish title vs registration.
- Know basic documentation needed.
- Understand renewal responsibility.
- Recognize consequences of failing to maintain valid documents.

## 1. Title vs Registration

Title proves ownership; registration permits lawful road operation and associates vehicle with plates & fees.

## 2. Documentation (Typical)

- Proof of ownership (previous title, bill of sale).
- Identification of owner.
- Safety/emissions (if applicable, verify state requirements).
- Insurance proof.
- Fee payment.

## 3. Renewal & Updates

Registrations expire; timely renewal avoids penalties. Update address, ownership changes promptly.

## 4. Non-Compliance Consequences

- Fines or citations.
- Potential towing or impound.
- Delays in future transactions.

## 5. Best Practices

- Calendar reminders for renewal.
- Maintain organized document file.
- Promptly resolve address changes.

## 3 things to remember

- Title ≠ Registration: one proves ownership, the other permits operation.
- Keep insurance proof and address up to date for registrations.
- Use calendar reminders to renew before expiration.
