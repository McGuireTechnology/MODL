## Scenario / Think & Reveal

<details><summary>1. Dense urban traffic; frequent sudden stops. What adjustments?</summary>
Increase following gap, heighten scan frequency, smooth speed changes.
</details>

<details><summary>2. Driver tailgating; you have open lane right. Action?</summary>
Signal, change lane when safe, let driver pass.
</details>

<details><summary>3. Spot pedestrian near curb looking over shoulder. Anticipation?</summary>
Prepare for sudden crossing; reduce speed slightly, cover brake.
</details>

<details><summary>4. Planning lane change; car ahead brakes suddenly. Response?</summary>
Abort lane change, stabilize speed, re-assess after hazard clears.
</details>

<details><summary>5. Parallel parked vehicles with occupants visible. Hazard?</summary>
Possible door opening; increase lateral clearance.
</details>
