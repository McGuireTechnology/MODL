## Section 1: License & Legal Requirements

**1. At what age can you apply for an Instruction Permit in Missouri?**

- [ ] A) 14 years old
- [ ] B) 15 years old
- [ ] C) 16 years old
- [ ] D) 17 years old

??? success "Answer"
    **B) 15 years old** - Missouri allows teens to apply for an instruction permit at age 15.

---

**2. How many hours of behind-the-wheel driving practice are required during the Intermediate License stage?**

- [ ] A) 20 hours
- [ ] B) 30 hours
- [ ] C) 40 hours
- [ ] D) 50 hours

??? success "Answer"
    **C) 40 hours** - The GDL program requires 40 hours of supervised driving practice, including 10 hours at night.

---

**3. What documents are required to obtain a driver's license in Missouri? (Select the BEST answer)**

- [ ] A) Birth certificate only
- [ ] B) Social Security card only
- [ ] C) Proof of identity, Social Security number, and Missouri residency
- [ ] D) School ID and utility bill

??? success "Answer"
    **C) Proof of identity, Social Security number, and Missouri residency** - All three types of documents are required.

---

## Section 2: Traffic Signs & Signals

**4. What does a red octagon sign mean?**

- [ ] A) Yield
- [ ] B) Stop
- [ ] C) Do Not Enter
- [ ] D) Wrong Way

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Stop** - The octagon (8-sided) shape is unique to stop signs.

---

**5. A flashing red traffic signal should be treated as:**

- [ ] A) A stop sign
- [ ] B) A yield sign
- [ ] C) Caution, proceed carefully
- [ ] D) The signal is broken, ignore it

??? success "Answer"
    **A) A stop sign** - Come to a complete stop, yield to traffic, then proceed when safe.

---

**6. What does a solid yellow center line mean?**

- [ ] A) Passing is permitted in either direction
- [ ] B) No passing allowed on your side
- [ ] C) Two-way traffic ahead
- [ ] D) Right turn only

??? success "Answer"
    **B) No passing allowed on your side** - A solid yellow line on your side means do not pass.

---

**7. At a green arrow traffic signal, you should:**

- [ ] A) Stop and wait
- [ ] B) Proceed with caution after yielding
- [ ] C) Proceed in the direction of the arrow; oncoming traffic is stopped
- [ ] D) Treat it like a yield sign

??? success "Answer"
    **C) Proceed in the direction of the arrow; oncoming traffic is stopped** - Green arrow means protected movement.

---

## Section 3: Rules of the Road

**8. When two vehicles arrive at an uncontrolled intersection at the same time, who has the right-of-way?**

- [ ] A) The vehicle on the left
- [ ] B) The vehicle on the right
- [ ] C) The larger vehicle
- [ ] D) The faster vehicle

??? success "Answer"
    **B) The vehicle on the right** - When in doubt, yield to the right.

---

**9. What is the speed limit in a school zone when children are present?**

- [ ] A) 15 mph
- [ ] B) 20 mph
- [ ] C) 25 mph
- [ ] D) 30 mph

??? success "Answer"
    **B) 20 mph** - School zones are 20 mph when children are present or signs indicate school is in session.

---

**10. When following another vehicle, you should maintain a following distance of at least:**

- [ ] A) 1 second
- [ ] B) 2 seconds
- [ ] C) 3 seconds
- [ ] D) 5 seconds

??? success "Answer"
    **C) 3 seconds** - Use the 3-second rule under normal conditions. Increase in bad weather or heavy traffic.

---

**11. You must use your headlights:**

- [ ] A) From sunset to sunrise only
- [ ] B) Whenever you use your windshield wipers
- [ ] C) When visibility is less than 500 feet
- [ ] D) All of the above

??? success "Answer"
    **D) All of the above** - Headlights are required from sunset to sunrise, when using wipers, and when visibility is reduced.

---

## Section 4: Parking

**12. When parking on a hill without a curb, you should turn your wheels:**

- [ ] A) Straight ahead
- [ ] B) Toward the shoulder of the road
- [ ] C) Away from the shoulder
- [ ] D) It doesn't matter

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Toward the shoulder of the road** - This allows the vehicle to roll away from traffic if brakes fail.

---

**13. You cannot park within how many feet of a fire hydrant?**

- [ ] A) 5 feet
- [ ] B) 10 feet
- [ ] C) 15 feet
- [ ] D) 20 feet

??? success "Answer"
    **C) 15 feet** - Must maintain at least 15 feet from a fire hydrant.

---

## Section 5: Highway Driving

**14. When entering a highway from an entrance ramp, you should:**

- [ ] A) Stop and wait for an opening
- [ ] B) Enter at or near the speed of traffic
- [ ] C) Enter slowly and speed up gradually
- [ ] D) Sound your horn to alert other drivers

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Enter at or near the speed of traffic** - Match the flow of traffic for a safe merge.

---

**15. If you miss your exit on a highway, you should:**

- [ ] A) Stop and back up
- [ ] B) Make a U-turn when safe
- [ ] C) Continue to the next exit
- [ ] D) Drive across the median

??? success "Answer"
    **C) Continue to the next exit** - Never stop, back up, or make dangerous maneuvers on a highway.

---

## Section 6: Sharing the Road

**16. When a school bus has its red lights flashing and stop arm extended, you must:**

- [ ] A) Slow down and proceed with caution
- [ ] B) Stop at least 10 feet away
- [ ] C) Stop only if you're behind the bus
- [ ] D) Change lanes to pass

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Stop at least 10 feet away** - Stop for a school bus with flashing red lights, both directions (unless divided highway).

---

**17. When sharing the road with large trucks, you should avoid:**

- [ ] A) Their blind spots (No-Zones)
- [ ] B) Following too closely
- [ ] C) Cutting in front of them
- [ ] D) All of the above

??? success "Answer"
    **D) All of the above** - Large trucks have large blind spots, need more stopping distance, and require extra space.

---

## Section 7: Special Conditions

**18. When driving in fog, you should:**

- [ ] A) Use high beam headlights
- [ ] B) Use low beam headlights
- [ ] C) Turn off your headlights
- [ ] D) Use parking lights only

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Use low beam headlights** - High beams reflect off fog and reduce visibility.

---

**19. If your vehicle begins to hydroplane, you should:**

- [ ] A) Brake firmly
- [ ] B) Accelerate to get through the water
- [ ] C) Ease off the gas and steer straight
- [ ] D) Turn sharply to one side

??? success "Answer"
    **C) Ease off the gas and steer straight** - Let the vehicle slow naturally and maintain control.

---

## Section 8: Impaired & Distracted Driving

**20. Missouri law prohibits drivers under 21 from:**

- [ ] A) Using a handheld phone while driving
- [ ] B) Driving with any detectable amount of alcohol
- [ ] C) Driving between midnight and 5 AM
- [ ] D) Having more than one passenger

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Driving with any detectable amount of alcohol** - Zero tolerance law for drivers under 21.

---

**21. Sending or reading a text message while driving is:**

- [ ] A) Legal if under 21 years old
- [ ] B) Illegal for all drivers in Missouri
- [ ] C) Legal if at a red light
- [ ] D) Only illegal for commercial drivers

??? success "Answer"
    **B) Illegal for all drivers in Missouri** - Texting while driving is prohibited for all drivers in Missouri.

---

## Section 9: Point System

**22. How many points will result in a license suspension for a driver under 18?**

- [ ] A) 6 points in 12 months
- [ ] B) 8 points in 18 months
- [ ] C) 10 points in 24 months
- [ ] D) 12 points in 12 months

??? success "Answer"
    **B) 8 points in 18 months** - Drivers under 18 face suspension at 8 points in 18 months (adults: 8 points in 18 months, 12 points in 12 months, or 18 points in 24 months).

---

**23. Speeding 20 mph over the limit results in how many points?**

- [ ] A) 2 points
- [ ] B) 3 points
- [ ] C) 4 points
- [ ] D) 6 points

??? success "Answer"
    **C) 4 points** - Excessive speeding (20+ mph over limit) is 4 points.

---

## Section 10: Vehicle Equipment

**24. Safety belts must be worn by:**

- [ ] A) Driver only
- [ ] B) Front seat passengers only
- [ ] C) All occupants of the vehicle
- [ ] D) Only passengers under 18

??? success "Answer"
    **C) All occupants of the vehicle** - Missouri law requires all occupants to wear seat belts.

---

**25. Your vehicle must be inspected every:**

- [ ] A) 6 months
- [ ] B) 1 year
- [ ] C) 2 years
- [ ] D) 3 years

??? success "Answer"
    **C) 2 years** - Missouri requires safety inspections every two years (or annually for certain vehicles).
