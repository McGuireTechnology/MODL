## Full Practice Test

<quiz>
At what age can you apply for an Instruction Permit in Missouri?
- [ ] 14 years old
- [x] 15 years old
- [ ] 16 years old
- [ ] 17 years old

Missouri allows teens to apply for an instruction permit at age 15.
</quiz>

<quiz>
How many hours of behind-the-wheel driving practice are required during the Intermediate License stage?
- [ ] 20 hours
- [ ] 30 hours
- [x] 40 hours
- [ ] 50 hours

The GDL program requires 40 hours of supervised driving practice, including 10 hours at night.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What documents are required to obtain a driver's license in Missouri?
- [ ] Birth certificate only
- [ ] Social Security card only
- [x] Proof of identity, Social Security number, and Missouri residency
- [ ] School ID and utility bill

All three types of documents are required: proof of identity, Social Security number, and Missouri residency.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What does a red octagon sign mean?
- [ ] Yield
- [x] Stop
- [ ] Do Not Enter
- [ ] Wrong Way

The octagon (8-sided) shape is unique to stop signs.
</quiz>

<quiz>
A flashing red traffic signal should be treated as:
- [x] A stop sign
- [ ] A yield sign
- [ ] Caution, proceed carefully
- [ ] The signal is broken, ignore it

Come to a complete stop, yield to traffic, then proceed when safe - just like a stop sign.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What does a solid yellow center line on your side mean?
- [ ] Passing is permitted in either direction
- [x] No passing allowed on your side
- [ ] Two-way traffic ahead
- [ ] Right turn only

A solid yellow line on your side means do not pass.
</quiz>

<quiz>
At a green arrow traffic signal, you should:
- [ ] Stop and wait
- [ ] Proceed with caution after yielding
- [x] Proceed in the direction of the arrow; oncoming traffic is stopped
- [ ] Treat it like a yield sign

Green arrow means protected movement - oncoming traffic is held by a red signal.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When two vehicles arrive at an uncontrolled intersection at the same time, who has the right-of-way?
- [ ] The vehicle on the left
- [x] The vehicle on the right
- [ ] The larger vehicle
- [ ] The faster vehicle

When in doubt, yield to the right.
</quiz>

<quiz>
What is the speed limit in a school zone when children are present?
- [ ] 15 mph
- [x] 20 mph
- [ ] 25 mph
- [ ] 30 mph

School zones are 20 mph when children are present or signs indicate school is in session.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When following another vehicle, you should maintain a following distance of at least:
- [ ] 1 second
- [ ] 2 seconds
- [x] 3 seconds
- [ ] 5 seconds

Use the 3-second rule under normal conditions. Increase in bad weather or heavy traffic.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You must use your headlights:
- [ ] From sunset to sunrise only
- [ ] Whenever you use your windshield wipers
- [ ] When visibility is less than 500 feet
- [x] All of the above

Headlights are required from sunset to sunrise, when using wipers, and when visibility is reduced.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When parking on a hill without a curb, you should turn your wheels:
- [ ] Straight ahead
- [x] Toward the shoulder of the road
- [ ] Away from the shoulder
- [ ] It doesn't matter

This allows the vehicle to roll away from traffic if brakes fail.
</quiz>

<quiz>
You cannot park within how many feet of a fire hydrant?
- [ ] 5 feet
- [ ] 10 feet
- [x] 15 feet
- [ ] 20 feet

Must maintain at least 15 feet from a fire hydrant.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When entering a highway from an entrance ramp, you should:
- [ ] Stop and wait for an opening
- [x] Enter at or near the speed of traffic
- [ ] Enter slowly and speed up gradually
- [ ] Sound your horn to alert other drivers

Match the flow of traffic for a safe merge.
</quiz>

<quiz>
If you miss your exit on a highway, you should:
- [ ] Stop and back up
- [ ] Make a U-turn when safe
- [x] Continue to the next exit
- [ ] Drive across the median

Never stop, back up, or make dangerous maneuvers on a highway.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When a school bus has its red lights flashing and stop arm extended, you must:
- [ ] Slow down and proceed with caution
- [x] Stop at least 10 feet away
- [ ] Stop only if you're behind the bus
- [ ] Change lanes to pass

Stop for a school bus with flashing red lights, both directions (unless divided highway).
</quiz>

<quiz>
When sharing the road with large trucks, you should avoid:
- [ ] Their blind spots (No-Zones)
- [ ] Following too closely
- [ ] Cutting in front of them
- [x] All of the above

Large trucks have large blind spots, need more stopping distance, and require extra space.
</quiz>

<quiz>
When driving in fog, you should:
- [ ] Use high beam headlights
- [x] Use low beam headlights
- [ ] Turn off your headlights
- [ ] Use parking lights only

High beams reflect off fog and reduce visibility. Use low beams.
</quiz>

<quiz>
If your vehicle begins to hydroplane, you should:
- [ ] Brake firmly
- [ ] Accelerate to get through the water
- [x] Ease off the gas and steer straight
- [ ] Turn sharply to one side

Let the vehicle slow naturally and maintain control.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Missouri law prohibits drivers under 21 from:
- [ ] Using a handheld phone while driving
- [x] Driving with any detectable amount of alcohol
- [ ] Driving between midnight and 5 AM
- [ ] Having more than one passenger

Zero tolerance law - drivers under 21 cannot have any detectable amount of alcohol.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Sending or reading a text message while driving is:
- [ ] Legal if under 21 years old
- [x] Illegal for all drivers in Missouri
- [ ] Legal if at a red light
- [ ] Only illegal for commercial drivers

Texting while driving is prohibited for all drivers in Missouri.
</quiz>

<quiz>
How many points will result in a license suspension for a driver under 18?
- [ ] 6 points in 12 months
- [x] 8 points in 18 months
- [ ] 10 points in 24 months
- [ ] 12 points in 12 months

Drivers under 18 face suspension at 8 points in 18 months.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Speeding 20 mph over the limit results in how many points?
- [ ] 2 points
- [ ] 3 points
- [x] 4 points
- [ ] 6 points

Excessive speeding (20+ mph over limit) is 4 points.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Safety belts must be worn by:
- [ ] Driver only
- [ ] Front seat passengers only
- [x] All occupants of the vehicle
- [ ] Only passengers under 18

Missouri law requires all occupants to wear seat belts.
</quiz>

<quiz>
Your vehicle must be inspected every:
- [ ] 6 months
- [ ] 1 year
- [x] 2 years
- [ ] 3 years

Missouri requires safety inspections every two years (or annually for certain vehicles).
</quiz>
