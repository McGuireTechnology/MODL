# Mandatory Insurance (Chapter 13)

!!! abstract "TL;DR"
    Insurance proves financial responsibility; avoid lapses and keep proof handy—post-incident documentation helps claims.

## Scope

Foundations of financial responsibility, insurance proof, lapses, and incident handling. Verify Missouri minimum coverage figures.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand purpose of mandatory insurance.
- Recognize consequences of lapses.
- Know basic post-incident documentation needs.
- Implement strategies to maintain continuous coverage.

## 1. Financial Responsibility

Insurance provides liability protection; required minimums ensure base compensation capability (confirm actual limits in official materials).

## 2. Proof of Insurance

Carry physical or digital card; must present during traffic stops, registration, certain crashes.

## 3. Lapses & Consequences

- Possible fines.
- Registration or license impacts.
- Increased future premiums.

## 4. Maintaining Coverage

- Automatic payment setup.
- Promptly update address or vehicle changes.
- Periodic policy review for adequate limits.

## 5. After an Incident

- Ensure safety, exchange information.
- Document: time, location, conditions.
- Contact insurer promptly.

## 3 things to remember

- Minimums set a floor, not a ceiling—limits can be increased for protection.
- Address/payment updates prevent accidental lapses.
- After a crash: ensure safety, exchange info, document details, notify insurer.
