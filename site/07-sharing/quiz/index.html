    True / False¶

    #

    Yellow bus lights = imminent stop.

    Prepare to stop.

    #

    Motorcycles require less following distance due to size.

    They can stop quickly; maintain gap.

    #

    Opening doors can intrude.

    #

    Protects workers/emergency responders.

    #

    Remaining in truck blind spot is acceptable if signaling.

    Exit blind spot promptly.

    Scenario / Think & Reveal¶

    1. Opposite side of divided highway bus with red lights. Stop? Likely exempt if physical median present—verify state rule; on undivided road you must stop.
    2. Cyclist signals left, traffic moderate. Your action? Allow lane change, slow slightly, avoid overtaking during maneuver.
    3. Motorcycle ahead with constant turn signal. Interpretation? Do not assume turn; maintain gap until movement confirms.
    4. Large truck begins wide right turn from left lane. Response? Do not squeeze right; yield space until turn completes.
    5. Emergency vehicle in multi-lane congestion behind you. Strategy? Coordinate gradual lane shift right with surrounding drivers; avoid abrupt stop mid-lane.
