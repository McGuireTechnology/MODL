# Missouri Class F Driver License Study Workbook

This repository provides an original, paraphrased study workbook to help candidates prepare for the Missouri Class F (non-commercial) driver license knowledge examination. It deliberately excludes Commercial Driver License (CDL) specific material.

## Disclaimer

This content is an educational aid and not a substitute for the official Missouri Driver Guide published by the Missouri Department of Revenue (DOR). Always consult the latest official guide for authoritative rules, numbers, and procedural details. No verbatim reproduction of the guide is intended; all material here is paraphrased or synthesized from general U.S. driving principles and Missouri-specific public information. Laws can change.

## Structure

- `workbook/outline.md` – Modules & objectives
- `workbook/sections/` – Detailed study modules
- `workbook/quizzes/` – Per-module formative assessments
- `workbook/answer_key/` – Answers with explanations
- `workbook/exam/` – Full-length practice exam
- `workbook/study_tips.md` – Strategy & schedule suggestions

## Suggested Use

1. Read each section actively; annotate with personal examples.
2. Complete the quiz immediately after a module (spaced repetition).
3. Revisit missed questions and locate the concept in the section.
4. Take the full practice exam under timed conditions (target: 25 minutes).
5. Review all explanations, especially for guessed correct answers.

## Modules Overview

1. Licensing & Identification
2. Vehicle Equipment & Registration Basics
3. Traffic Control Devices (signs/signals/markings)
4. Right-of-Way & Lane Use
5. Speed, Passing & Following Distance
6. Turns, Intersections & Parking
7. Sharing the Road (vulnerable users & special vehicles)
8. Alcohol, Drugs & Impaired Driving Laws
9. Defensive & Emergency Driving
10. Points, Violations & License Actions
